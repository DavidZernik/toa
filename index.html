F.A.Q.


How do I obtain these tax credits?

TOA will provide the appropriate tax reports, depending on the type of credit earned, for the appropriate tax year. These credits can be carried back to the previous year, applied in the tax year the credit was generated, or carried forward for twenty years.

How are the credits distributed?

If the business entity is a sole proprietorship, S-Corp, LLC, LLP or Partnership, the credit passes to the owner, shareholder, member or partner in the same manner as losses are allocated. In a C-Corp, the credits are used by the corporation. They offset federal income taxes and can be carried back to the prior year, or carried forward for twenty years.

Can my CPA do this for me?

Unless your CPA is filling the proper 8550 forms within 28 day to the proper state office. It is unlikely your company will be receiving the right value of credits.

Can we do these credits in-house?

Some of our clients have conducted this process in-house until they realized that they could achieve a more cost-effective approach by outsourcing these services to the experts at TOA. In addition to our fully dedicated staff we also have secure relationships with many state offices and can help secure many credits that may have otherwise been overlooked. We even offer an audit to show you just how much you could be saving.

Will this be alot of work for our HR department?

By using our technology and process, paperwork becomes minimal. We also take on the burden of processing, tracking regulations, and identifying and calculating the credits.
