About us

At Tax Opportunities of America, we give our clients the opportunity to take advantage of all the possible benefits due them, including benefits dependent on specific jurisdictions and locations. We ensure you are capitalizing on programs that may be unique to your industry, saving you potentially thousands of dollars.

We pride ourselves in complete transparency while navigating through the process of claiming the credit, and we only get paid if you successfully claim tax credits.

Potentially eligible categories include

  • Qualified veterans

  • Vocational rehabilitation referrals

  • Supplemental security income recipients

  • Qualified long-term unemployed

  • Temporary assistance for needy families recipients

  • Summer youth employees

  • Residents of empowerment zones or rural renewal counties
